The biodiversity estimator is based on the association probability between vernacular names and botanical species. The association probability is estimated by a Dirichlet trial based on the vernacular/botanical association frequency observed in the whole inventory. This association probability then allows generating theoretical inventories with complete botanical identification with the assignment of botanical species to trees with only a vernacular name. The repetition of the replacement process produces a set of theoretical inventories which diversities gives the probability distribution of the initial inventory diversity.
